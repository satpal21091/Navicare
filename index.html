Our Story.

We’re disability support company from Melbourne

Navicare is a family-owned business and was established to make a difference within the disability sector. Navicare believes in providing people living with a disability absolute freedom of choice regarding their support needs. Collectively, Navicare has a range of experience within the disability and mental health sectors, as well as within the social work. Navicare intends to provide high-quality and accessible services that will improve the quality of life for people living with a disability.

OUR Mission, Vision and Values.

We provide a complete list of best support services

Navicare’s Vision is to support people with disability to gain control over personal choices for healthy functioning or better outcomes Navicare’s Mission is to understand the requisite from people with disability to make the services accessible and easily approachable which align with our vision of helping people gain control over their choices. All of Navicare’s operations and activities are built upon the Values of:

Reasonable accommodation

We aim to have a person-centred approach and ensure people with disability equally enjoy their rights as other people. We provide reasonable accommodation with necessary and appropriate modification as according to the individual needs.

Independent living

We remove the barriers for socially inclusive community involvement to make sure people with disabilities have access to services in the same way as others.

Developing skills

We continuously strengthen our services for people with disabilities to develop skills and fully participate in the community.

Respect

We respect an individual's choices and their freedom to gain control over their life. We adopt a person-centred approach to our work, and we foster an environment of mutual respect and trust among ourselves and all with whom we serve.

Transparency

We tell the truth and strive to earn the trust of those around us.

Innovation

We seek ideas and approaches that can change the way the world discovers, teaches, and heals, and we are committed to lifelong learning.

Accountability

We are each responsible for ensuring the organisation always maintains high standards and achieves high success levels.

Service Through Teamwork

We collaborate with each other to serve our clients and our community effectively and compassionately.
